About Brindle

Brindle is currently a Scenic Design MFA candidate at the University of Idaho in Moscow. Her recent projects include designs for adaptations of A Christmas Carol, The Revolutionists, and Little Women. As an exit project for her Spring 2022 graduation, Brindle will be designing the set for Mr. Burns, a Post-Electric Play.

After graduating with a BFA in Theatre from Weber State University in 2018, Brindle took a year off from school to work for the Tuacahn Center for the Arts. While employed there as a scenic painter, she had the opportunity to build and paint productions including The Prince of Egypt, Matilda, and Cinderella. Brindle looks forward to seeing where future opportunities might take her.

On the rare occasions that she has free time, Brindle enjoys watching horror movies, nerding out over Greek mythology, and spending time with her dog, cat, and husband (usually in that order).
